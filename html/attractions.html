Nanjing Attractions

Discover the human and natural beauty of the ancient capital of the Six Dynasties Nanjing Attractions Introduction

Fuzi Temple

Historic Relics

Nanjing Fuzi Temple is a masterpiece of ancient Chinese architectural complex, and is the largest cultural temple complex in the south of the Yangtze River.

Opening time：8:30-22:00 Price：￥40

Xuanwu Lake

Natural Scence

Xuanwu Lake is the largest inner-city lake in Nanjing and the largest royal garden lake in China.

Opening time：All Day Price：￥30

Zhongshan Mausoleum

Historic Relics

Zhongshan Mausoleum is the mausoleum of Dr Sun Yat-sen, the great forerunner of China's modern democratic revolution.

Opening time: 8:30-18:00 Price：Free

Nanjing Museum

Humanistic Landscape

One of the three major museums in China, with a large collection of valuable relics and artefacts.

Opening time：9:00-17:00 Price:Free

Jiming Temple

Historic Relics

Nanjing Jiming Temple, the first temple of the Southern Dynasties, is located in Beijing East Road, Xuanwu District, with a history of more than 1,700 years.

Opening time：9:00-17:00 Price：￥10

Meiling Palace

Historic Relics

Nanjing Meiling Palace, the first villa in the Far East, is located in Zhongshan Scenic Area, the former residence of Chiang Kai-shek and Soong Meiling.

Opening time：9:00-17:00 Price：￥30
