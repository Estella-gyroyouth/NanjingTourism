Recommended Itinerary

First Day

09:00

Zhongshan Mausoleum

It is recommended to visit for 2 hours, you can enjoy the Zhongshan Mausoleum complex and the natural landscape of Zhongshan Scenic Area.

14:00

Presidential Palace

Suggested visit 2.5 hours, learn about the modern history, appreciate the architectural style of Chinese and Western.

19:00

Fuzi Temple-Qinhuai River

In the evening, visit the Qinhuai River, experience the night view of the ancient city and taste the local snacks.
